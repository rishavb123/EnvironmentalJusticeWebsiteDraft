What is Environmental Justice

As a Environmental Justice in the 21st Century: Race still Matters by Robert D. Bullard states
The environmental justice framework attempts to uncover the underlying assumptions that may contribute to and produce unequal protection

Why should you care?

Environmental Justice is something that effects everyone. While the effect might not be direct, it is effecting people everywhere, even here in Atlanta. It is necessary for us to take action for environmental justice for the better of our community.

How can you get involved?

See our events and programs pages, where we have multiple opportunities that you can get involved in. The events are generally one time things, with no long term commitments while the programs are recurring and require more commitment.
